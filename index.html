Aim

To measure the angle of a triangular bar accurately using a bevel protractor.

Introduction

A bevel protractor is a precision instrument used to measure and set angles. It consists of a base, a circular protractor dial graduated in degrees, and a vernier scale that allows readings up to minutes of a degree. It is commonly used in tool rooms and workshops to measure the angle between two surfaces of a workpiece.
